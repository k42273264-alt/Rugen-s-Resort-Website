Island Adventures

Experience the Beauty of Rügen

The Sunday Resort Rügen is located on the Jasmund peninsula near the famous chalk cliffs and the Jasmund National Park. From beautiful beaches to scenic hiking trails, the island offers unforgettable experiences in every season.

Top Attractions

Discover the Region

Jasmund National Park

Home to the famous chalk cliffs and UNESCO beech forests, offering spectacular coastal views.

Schaabe Beach

A 12 km long sandy beach – the longest beach on the Baltic Sea coast.

Rügen Chalk Museum

Interactive exhibition about chalk mining and fossils from the Cretaceous period.

Störtebeker Festival

One of Germany’s most spectacular open-air theatre events with pirates, ships and fireworks.

Cycling & Hiking

Explore the island by bike or follow scenic hiking trails through forests and along the cliffs.
