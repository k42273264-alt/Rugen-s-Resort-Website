A Relaxing Vacation Village

The vacation village at Sunday Resort Rügen offers 86 cozy apartments in two-story houses surrounded by expansive lawns and peaceful countryside. The apartments provide comfortable living spaces and are just a few minutes walk from the hotel facilities.
