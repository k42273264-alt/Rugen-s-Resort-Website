A Unique Venue for Your Event

The Sunday Resort Rügen offers the ideal setting for conferences, seminars and special events. Located in the beautiful landscape of Germany's largest island, our resort combines modern event spaces with historic surroundings and outstanding hospitality.

Whether you choose our neo-baroque mansion built in 1911, the large garden areas or our fully equipped meeting rooms, your event will have the perfect backdrop.
