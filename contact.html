Getting Here

Travel to Sunday Resort Rügen

Nearest train station: Sagard – about 10 minutes from the resort.

Drive via Stralsund across the Rügen bridge and follow signs towards Sagard and Jasmund National Park.

Nearest airports: Rostock–Laage Airport or Berlin Brandenburg Airport.
