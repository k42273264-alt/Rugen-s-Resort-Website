Families

Vacations for Young and Old

Sunday Resort Rügen is designed especially for families. Surrounded by wide meadows and fresh country air, the resort offers a variety of activities such as an indoor playground, adventure pool and children’s programs to ensure that young guests and adults alike enjoy an unforgettable stay.

Activities

Fun for the Whole Family


Indoor Playground

A large indoor play area with climbing tower, trampoline and games.

Adventure Pool

Splash Erlebniswelt water park with slides and family pools.

Kids Club

Villa Kunterbunt offers activities, games and nature adventures for children.

Outdoor Activities

Mini golf, football field, beach volleyball and cycling tours.

Explore Outdoors

Cycling

Explore the island of Rügen by bike. Bicycles can be rented at the Splash reception desk and the resort is a perfect starting point for cycling tours.

Sports & Games

Enjoy mini golf, soccer and beach volleyball courts located in the outdoor recreation area.
