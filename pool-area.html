Splash Erlebniswelt

Swimming Fun for Everyone

Covering more than 3,500 m², the Splash Erlebniswelt water park offers a unique experience for families and relaxation seekers. The indoor area features a heated pool with a lazy river, bubble loungers, waterfall showers, an 80-meter water slide and a separate children's pool.
