A Culinary Journey

At Sunday Resort Rügen our guests enjoy high-quality seasonal, regional and international cuisine. From buffet dining to Italian specialties and relaxing drinks at the bar, there is something for every taste.

Gutsherrenhaus

Built in 1911, the Gutsherrenhaus restaurant offers seasonal, regional and international delicacies prepared with great attention to detail in a unique historic setting.

Open seasonally

Restaurant Hofküche

Breakfast and evening buffet with international cuisine and regional specialties such as Baltic Sea pike-perch.

Open daily 7:00 a.m. – 1:00 a.m.

L'Osteria

The Italian restaurant brings Mediterranean cuisine and romantic candlelight dinners to your holiday.

Open seasonally

Hotel Bar “Zur Tränke”

Enjoy cocktails, wine by the fireplace or a freshly tapped beer.

Open daily 2:00 p.m. – 1:00 a.m.

Pool Bistro “Snack & Co.”

Located by the pool area, the bistro serves snacks, salads, light meals and delicious ice cream specialties.

Open daily 12:00 p.m. – 7:00 p.m.
