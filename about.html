Our Story

Sunday Resort Rügen

A Resort Surrounded by Nature

Sunday Resort Rügen welcomes guests to the beautiful island of Rügen, located on the scenic Jasmund Peninsula near the famous chalk cliffs and Jasmund National Park. The resort combines relaxation, nature and family-friendly hospitality in a unique atmosphere.

A Destination for Every Guest

The spacious resort features stylish hotel rooms, comfortable vacation apartments and a wide range of leisure facilities including the Splash Erlebniswelt water park, wellness spa and several restaurants. Couples, families and active travelers alike can discover their perfect holiday experience.

Location

Island of Rügen

Located on Germany’s largest island, Sunday Resort Rügen is surrounded by forests, beaches and the natural beauty of the Baltic Sea coast.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Jasmund National Park
  • Königsstuhl Chalk Cliffs
  • Schaabe Beach – 12 km sandy coastline
  • Rügen Chalk Museum
  • Störtebeker Festival in Ralswiek

Why Guests Love Our Location

  • Close to national parks and nature trails
  • Perfect starting point for cycling tours
  • Family-friendly attractions nearby
  • One of the sunniest regions in Germany
